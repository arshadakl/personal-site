    About me

    My name is Arshad, and I'm a skilled male web developer and graphics designer hailing from the beautiful state of Kerala. Following my high school graduation, I embarked on a transformative journey by joining a prestigious brocamp hosted by the renowned company, Brototype. This invaluable experience served as the springboard for honing my knowledge and skill set in the world of web development.

    During my time at the brocamp, I delved deep into the realms of coding and design, immersing myself in the intricacies of the MERN stack. As a web developer and graphics designer, I find immense joy in blending creativity with technical prowess to bring innovative ideas to life. My passion for designing visually captivating graphics and building intuitive, seamless websites has been the driving force behind my continuous growth in the field. With a strong foundation laid by the brocamp and my unwavering determination, I am excited to embark on new challenges and ventures, using my skills to create impactful digital experiences that leave a lasting impression on users.
